Welcome to QuizStream!

Thank you for joining us! QuizStream is aimed at making learning more engaging by turning YouTube videos into interactive quizzes. Whether you're a teacher, a content creator or just someone who loves to learn, QuizStream simplifies the process of creating quizzes based on video content.

What Can QuizStream Do for You?

With QuizStream, all you need is a YouTube video that you'd like to turn into a quiz. The application does the heavy lifting for you, automatically generating questions from the video's content. You can customize the quiz by setting the difficulty level and by choosing the language.

To make this magic happen, QuizStream uses a language model to analyze the video transcript. You'll need an API key of some LLM service to use this feature, but don't worry—getting started is easy, and we've even included an API key to help you try it out right away.

How Does QuizStream Work?

We've built QuizStream with a modern and user-friendly design to make your experience smooth and enjoyable. Here’s how it all comes together:

  • Easy-to-Use Interface: Our sleek Angular-based frontend makes navigating and using QuizStream a breeze.
  • Powerful Backend:We use Spring Boot to handle all the data and requests behind the scenes.
  • Smart AI Processing: Our Python-based AI service converts video content into quiz questions effortlessly.
  • Reliable Data Storage: Your quizzes and user information are safely stored in a PostgreSQL database.

Why Choose QuizStream?

QuizStream is perfect for anyone looking to make learning more interactive and fun. Whether you're preparing for a class, creating content or simply testing your knowledge, QuizStream makes it easy to create quizzes with minimal effort.

Best of all, QuizStream is open-source! We welcome you to explore the project on GitHub, where you can see how it works, suggest improvements or even contribute to its development. Check out QuizStream on GitHub and become part of our community!
